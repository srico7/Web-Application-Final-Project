The life on land

A prosperous life on earth is the basis of our life on earth. We are all part of the Earth's escosystem and we have caused severe damaged to it due to deforestation, loss of natural habitat and land degradation. Promoting the sustainable use of our escosystems and conserving biodiversity is not a factor. It is the key to our existence.

Protect, restore and promote

Terrestrial ecosystems provide a variety of goods, such as raw materials for construction and energy, food and a variety of ecosystem services, such as carbon capture, water quality maintenance, water flow regulation, and erosion control, all of which help to reduce the risk of natural disasters like floods.
