SUMMARY OF THE PROJECT

The primary objective of the web site is to detect the current problems and find solutions to overcome with sustainable environment. SDG 15 looks for the upkeep of existence on land and priorities which is established through international conventions and agreements.

The natural forest area loss and biodiversity decline as opposed to the restrained sources is a massive area that we target to answer. We inspire conservation and improvement specialists to have interaction with the ones answerable for all of the agenda 2030 objectives to make certain that Life on Land is a concern in all SDG associated processes. So basically, we are going to build a platform to discuss, share and to get certain information about SDG 15 Life on Land for anyone that are interested in.
